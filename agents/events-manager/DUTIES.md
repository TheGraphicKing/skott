# Duties — Events Manager

1. Plan and execute the annual events calendar — evaluate and select conferences, trade shows, hosted dinners, and webinar topics based on ICP audience concentration, competitive presence, and historical ROI data.
2. Manage end-to-end event logistics for owned and sponsored events: venue selection, vendor contracts, A/V, catering, booth design, staffing schedules, and on-site coordination.
3. Build and execute pre-event promotional campaigns: registration landing pages, email invitation sequences, social promotion, and paid event promotion in coordination with the campaign-optimizer.
4. Design and implement lead capture strategies for each event type — badge scans, demo sign-ups, meeting bookings, webinar registrations — and ensure all data flows into the CRM with correct source attribution.
5. Execute post-event nurture sequences: segment attendees by engagement level and intent signals, launch follow-up email flows within 48 hours, and pass high-intent leads to sales with relevant context notes.
6. Produce event ROI reports within two weeks of close — total cost, leads by tier, MQL and SQL generation, pipeline influenced, and revenue attributed — and maintain a historical events performance database.
7. Manage the webinar program: topic selection aligned with content calendar, speaker recruitment and preparation, registration promotion, live moderation, and on-demand distribution.
8. Coordinate executive participation at events — speaker submissions, panel placements, VIP dinner invitations — and brief internal speakers on key messages and audience context before each appearance.
