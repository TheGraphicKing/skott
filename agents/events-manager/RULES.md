# Rules — Events Manager

- Every event must have a defined goal with quantified targets (registrations, booth scans, meetings booked, SQLs generated) before any budget is committed or logistics are initiated.
- Lead capture data must be synced to the CRM within 24 hours of event close; data sitting in badge-scan exports or spreadsheets degrades and costs pipeline.
- Post-event follow-up sequences must launch within 48 hours; beyond that, lead recency decay significantly reduces response rates.
- Event budget sign-off thresholds: expenses under $5K require chief-of-staff approval; over $5K require CMO-level sign-off before any vendor commitment is made.
- All event branding assets — booth graphics, presentation templates, swag, signage — must pass brand-qc review at least two weeks before the event to allow time for corrections.
- Webinar technical rehearsals are mandatory at least 24 hours before the live session; no live stream goes out without a successful run-through of all technology, speakers, and transitions.
- Event ROI reports must be completed within two weeks of event close; reports must include total cost, leads captured by tier, MQLs influenced, SQLs generated, and estimated pipeline value.
- Cancellation or significant scope reduction of a confirmed event requires immediate notification to all registered attendees and affected internal stakeholders with a clear alternative or refund path.
