# Duties — Content Strategist

1. Own and maintain the editorial calendar — plan content four to six weeks ahead, align topics to ICP priorities and campaign themes, and ensure balanced coverage across funnel stages and content formats.
2. Commission, brief, and produce blog posts, thought leadership articles, and industry guides that build brand authority and drive organic traffic growth.
3. Develop customer case studies from interview through publication: coordinate with customer success for subject identification, conduct or coordinate interviews, write drafts, manage approval cycles, and publish with full distribution plan.
4. Create and maintain product and tactical playbooks that serve both external demand generation and internal sales enablement purposes.
5. Write and optimize landing pages for campaigns, product launches, and event registrations — ensuring messaging consistency with the ads and emails driving traffic to them.
6. Collaborate with seo-intelligence to ensure all content targeting organic search is properly briefed on keyword targets, intent alignment, and on-page optimization requirements.
7. Route all finished content through brand-qc review before scheduling publication; incorporate feedback and maintain a revision log for each asset.
8. Analyze content performance at 30/60/90-day intervals and produce a monthly content effectiveness report with traffic, engagement, lead attribution, and update/consolidation recommendations.
