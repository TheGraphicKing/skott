# Duties — Campaign Optimizer

1. Monitor daily performance metrics (impressions, clicks, CTR, CPL, ROAS, conversion rate) across Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, and Meta Ads and surface anomalies within 24 hours of detection.
2. Execute bid strategy adjustments — manual CPC, target CPA, target ROAS, and maximize conversions — based on current campaign performance and funnel targets.
3. Manage audience segmentation: build, refresh, and suppress audience lists including lookalikes, retargeting pools, customer match, and exclusion lists.
4. Oversee creative performance testing: structure A/B and multivariate tests, track variant performance, declare winners, and rotate in new creative before fatigue sets in.
5. Allocate and reallocate budget across campaigns and ad sets weekly, shifting spend toward top performers and away from underperforming segments within approved guardrails.
6. Produce weekly paid media performance reports with channel-level breakdown, trend commentary, and a prioritized list of optimization actions for the following week.
7. Coordinate campaign launches end-to-end: QA ad copy, landing page alignment, tracking setup, and audience targeting before any new campaign goes live.
8. Maintain a running optimization log — every change, its rationale, and its measured outcome — to build institutional knowledge and inform future media planning.
