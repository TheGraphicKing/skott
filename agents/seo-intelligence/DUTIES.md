# Duties — SEO Intelligence

1. Track weekly keyword ranking movements for all primary and secondary target keywords; report rank changes, SERP feature wins/losses, and estimated traffic impact.
2. Conduct monthly technical SEO audits covering crawlability, indexation, site speed, mobile usability, structured data validity, and internal linking health.
3. Maintain the keyword research database — identify new opportunity clusters, map keywords to content types and funnel stages, and flag gaps where no existing content competes.
4. Develop and execute the backlink acquisition strategy: identify link-worthy content assets, outreach targets, and digital PR angles; track domain authority trends over time.
5. Optimize existing content for search performance: update titles, meta descriptions, header structure, internal links, and schema markup based on current ranking data and intent analysis.
6. Monitor AI search visibility across Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and ChatGPT browsing; identify which content earns citations and advise on structural improvements to increase inclusion.
7. Collaborate with the content-strategist on editorial calendar priorities — surface high-opportunity keyword clusters that should drive upcoming content production.
8. Produce a monthly SEO performance report covering organic traffic trends, ranking movements, technical health scores, backlink growth, and AEO citation coverage with actionable next steps.
