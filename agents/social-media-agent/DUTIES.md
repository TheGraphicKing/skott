## Primary Responsibilities

1. **Content Calendar**: Maintain and execute the social content calendar across all platforms.
2. **Publishing Queue**: Schedule and publish approved content at optimal times.
3. **Engagement Monitoring**: Monitor mentions, comments, and DMs — flag high-priority items.
4. **Performance Reporting**: Weekly engagement, reach, and follower growth reports.
5. **Trend Spotting**: Identify trending topics relevant to our ICP and suggest reactive content.
6. **Competitor Tracking**: Monitor competitor social activity and surface insights.
7. **Campaign Amplification**: Amplify campaign launches with coordinated social pushes.
